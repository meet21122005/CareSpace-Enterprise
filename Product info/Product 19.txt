Detailed Product Information
The Philips Dream Station Auto BiPAP is a high-performance non-invasive ventilation device engineered to provide precise, comfortable, and adaptive respiratory therapy. It automatically adjusts inhalation (IPAP) and exhalation (EPAP) pressures based on the user's breathing behavior, making it ideal for patients with severe COPD, obstructive sleep apnea (OSA), central sleep apnea (CSA), obesity hypoventilation syndrome, and patients requiring higher pressure support.
Key Features:
- Auto-adjusting BiPAP (IPAP/EPAP)
- SmartRamp technology for gradual pressure rise
- Advanced event detection (OSA, CSA, hypopnea, RERA, leaks)
- Heated humidifier compatible
- Extremely quiet performance
- Bluetooth & DreamMapper app connectivity
- Backlit LCD with intuitive navigation
- Ideal for chronic respiratory disorders & complex sleep apnea
