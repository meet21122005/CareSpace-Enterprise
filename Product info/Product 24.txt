Detailed Product Information
The ResMed Lumis 150 is an advanced non-invasive ventilatory support device designed for patients requiring precise and adaptive respiratory therapy. It includes iVAPS (intelligent Volume-Assured Pressure Support) for maintaining stable ventilation during changing breathing patterns, AutoEPAP for automated airway pressure management, and state-of-the-art synchrony features such as TiControl and Trigger sensitivity adjustments.
Key Features:
- iVAPS for stable tidal volume
- AutoEPAP for automatic airway pressure control
- Adjustable backup respiratory rate
- TiControl for optimized patient-ventilator synchrony
- EPR for smooth pressure transitions
- Climate Control compatible humidification
- Leak management & comfort optimization
- Ideal for COPD, CSA, neuromuscular disorders, OHS, restrictive lung diseases & long-term home NIV therapy
