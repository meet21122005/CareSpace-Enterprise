The BiPAP A40 Pro is a high-performance non-invasive ventilator built for long-term respiratory therapy. It supports both adults and pediatrics, offering intelligent volume-assured pressure support, precise IPAP/EPAP control, and advanced monitoring. It is ideal for COPD, CSA, neuromuscular disorders, obesity hypoventilation syndrome (OHS), restrictive lung diseases, and home-ventilation programs.
Key Features:

- Spontaneous/Timed ventilation with backup RR
- Leak compensation algorithm for stable ventilation
- Integrated heated humidifier compatibility
- High pressure range (up to 30-40 cmH2O model dependent)
- Silent operation for night comfort
- Real-time patient monitoring & event tracking
- Suitable for chronic respiratory failure, neuromuscular disorders, and home ICU
