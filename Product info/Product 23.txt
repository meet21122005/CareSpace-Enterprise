Detailed Product Information
The ResMed Lumis 100 is an advanced non-invasive ventilatory support system that intelligently adapts to the user's breathing needs. It features ResMed's iVAPS (intelligent Volume-Assured Pressure Support) algorithm, AutoEPAP, and an advanced patient-ventilator syncing mechanism. This ensures stable ventilation throughout the night, even when the patient's breathing pattern naturally fluctuates.
Key Features:
- iVAPS (intelligent Volume-Assured Pressure Support)
- AutoEPAP to automatically adjust expiratory pressure
- Backup respiratory rate
- TiControl for improved patient-ventilator synchrony
- EPR (Expiratory Pressure Relief) for comfort
- Climate Control humidification compatibility
- Real-time respiratory event tracking
- Ideal for COPD, CSA, OHS, neuromuscular disorders & restrictive lung disease
