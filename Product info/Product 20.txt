Detailed Product Information
The Philips Dream Station BiPAP ST (Spontaneous/Timed) is engineered for patients who require both spontaneous breathing support and timed mandatory breaths. It ensures consistent ventilation even when the patient's natural breathing weakens or pauses. Perfect for conditions such as central sleep apnea (CSA), muscular dystrophy, obesity hypoventilation syndrome, COPD, and neuromuscular respiratory insufficiency.
Key Features:
- Spontaneous/Timed (S/T) mode for backup breaths
- Adjustable IPAP and EPAP pressures
- Backup respiratory rate for stable ventilation
- Heated humidifier compatible
- Advanced respiratory event detection
- Ultra-quiet motor for peaceful sleep
- Ramp and SmartRamp technology
- DreamMapper (Bluetooth) connectivity
- Ideal for complex sleep apnea & neuromuscular disorders
