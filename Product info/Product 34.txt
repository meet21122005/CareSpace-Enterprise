Detailed Product Information
The ETCO2 Monitor provides real-time capnography monitoring, displaying essential respiratory parameters such as end-tidal carbon dioxide levels and respiratory rate. This device is vital for evaluating ventilation effectiveness, detecting airway obstruction, monitoring sedation, and managing critical respiratory conditions.
It is widely used in ICUs, emergency departments, operating rooms, ambulances, post-anesthesia care units, and home ventilation setups. Designed with precision sensors, alarm systems, and a clear waveform display, it supports both mainstream and sidestream monitoring configurations.
Key Features:
- Real-time ETCO2 and respiratory rate monitoring
- High-precision capnography sensors
- Mainstream & sidestream options
- Audible & visual alarms
- High-brightness display screen
- Suitable for adults, pediatrics & neonates
- Ideal for anesthesia, emergency care, ventilator monitoring & sedation management
