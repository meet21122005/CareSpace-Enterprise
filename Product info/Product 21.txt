Detailed Product Information
The Philips Dream Station BiPAP AVAPS (Average Volume Assured Pressure Support) is designed to maintain a stable tidal volume by automatically adjusting pressure support. This makes it perfect for patients with variable respiratory needs, ensuring consistent ventilation throughout the night. Ideal for COPD, CSA, OHS, neuromuscular diseases such as ALS, and long-term chronic respiratory therapy.
Key Features:
- AVAPS mode for assured tidal volume
- Automatic pressure adjustments (IPAP/EPAP)
- Backup respiratory rate support
- Advanced event detection (apnea, CSA, leaks)
- Heated humidifier compatible
- SmartRamp for gradual comfort breath support
- Integrated Bluetooth (DreamMapper)
- Ideal for patients with fluctuating respiratory demands
