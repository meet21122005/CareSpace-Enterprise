Tubular Air Bed Mattress
Customer-Friendly Short Description
A durable, medical-grade tubular alternating air mattress designed to prevent pressure ulcers and provide continuous comfort for long-term bedridden and critical-care patients.
Detailed Product Information
The Tubular Air Bed Mattress features alternating air tubes that inflate and deflate in cycles to relieve pressure on skin tissue, improve circulation, and prevent bedsores for patients who are immobile or at high risk of developing pressure injuries. The reinforced PVC tubes ensure durability and can be individually replaced, extending product life.
Key Features:
- Alternating tubular air technology
- Medical-grade PVC air tubes
- Adjustable air pump with multiple pressure levels
- Enhanced airflow for skin protection
- Supports patients at high risk of bedsores
- Easy to clean and maintain
- Designed for long-term and elderly patients
